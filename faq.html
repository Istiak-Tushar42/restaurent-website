FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

DELIVERY

1. How do I order Delivery online?

Just follow these simple steps:

  •   a. Click on Order Now.
  •   b. Select Delivery.
  •   c. Enter your delivery address to Check Delivery Area.
  •   d. Select items from the Delivery Menu.
  •   e. Checkout, pay and we'll deliver!

Alternatively you can also call our Delivery Hotline at 01XXXXXXXXX from 10.00am - 10.30pm to place your order.

2. Can I make changes or cancel my order after placing an order?

You may change or cancel your order via our delivery hotline 01XXXXXXXXX, provided that the ordered items have not left the restaurant

3. How will I know if you has received my online order?

An order confirmation email will be sent to your registered email address once your order has been submitted.
(If you can’t find the email in your inbox, please check your ‘Spam/Junk’ folder or call our delivery hotline at 01XXXXXXXXX if you require further assistance)

4. What do I do if my order arrived with missing items?

Please call our delivery hotline at 01XXXXXXXXX to inform our customer service personnels of your missing items. We will arrange for the items to be delivered to you in the soonest time possible.

5. When is the operating hours for Delivery?

Delivery is available from 10.00am to 10.30pm from Mondays to Fridays including public holidays. However, delivery hours may vary depending on stores.

Please call our delivery hotline at 01XXXXXXXXX if you have further enquiries.

6. How much time does it take for KFC Delivery to arrive after placing an order?

We strive to deliver our service as fast as we can to meet your needs. The delivery time may vary depending on the status of delivery stores. An estimated time of delivery will be shown in every order you place online.

7. Is there a minimum order requirement for KFC Delivery?

A minimum order of RM15 excluding delivery charges applies to all orders on Delivery.

8. What are the payment options available for KFC Delivery?

The following payment options are available for KFC Delivery:

  • Cash (only for orders below RM300)
  • Credit Card (Visa & MasterCard)
  • Debit Card (Visa & MasterCard)
9. Can I schedule my order in advance?

You can place your future orders up to 3 days in advance via KFC Delivery Online. During the ordering process, select ‘Advance Order’ and choose your preferred delivery date and time.

To place orders beyond 3 days ahead, please call our delivery hotline at 01XXXXXXXXX (from 10.00am to 10.30pm.)

10. Can I order Delivery via the website without creating an account?

Yes, we now have a ‘guest checkout’ feature for customers who wish to place an order without creating an account.

However, we would recommend you to register with us for ease of all future orders. As a registered user, your details are saved and you will be able to select past orders in a single click. Furthermore, registration is free!

If you have further questions which are not listed above, please do not hesitate to contact our delivery hotline at 01XXXXXXXXX (from 10.00am – 10.30pm.)
